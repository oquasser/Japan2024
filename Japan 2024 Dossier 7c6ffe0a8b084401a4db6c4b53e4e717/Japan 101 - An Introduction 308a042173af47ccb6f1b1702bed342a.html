Japan 101 - An Introduction

The first trip that I made to Japan was the last time that I can remember having culture shock. If you’ve traveled in Europe, Central, or South America, the way of life and cultural norms are generally similar to our own and, thus, not too shocking. Japan is…different. In this section, I’ve tried to outline some things that either not found here in the U.S. or different from the way that we tend to do things. As a tourist, of course, you are afforded a certain leniency in having to follow certain nuances of etiquette, etc., but our Japanese hosts will certainly appreciate it if you display some prior knowledge and understanding of their culture.
