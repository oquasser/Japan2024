Useful Japanese Phrases

Most of our trip will be in the major urban centers of Japan, and more than likely, you’ll be able to get by without needing too much Japanese. Nonetheless, Japanese people will absolutely love it if you try to speak a little of their language - and, truly, a little will go a long way. I’ve listed out some important phrases below, including an attempt at explaining how to say them phonetically. Try some out while we’re in Tokyo in the beginning of the trip, since you may start needing them when we get to Nikko next!
