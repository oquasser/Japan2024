Kyoto to Osaka 10/28

Kyoto Station to Namba Station

Summary:

There are bullet trains leaving Kyoto Station every 15 ~ 25min going directly to Shin-Osaka station, arriving there in 13 minutes. From there, we’ll have to transfer to local trains to get close to our AirBnB (closest station: Kintetsu-Nippombashi). There are also local/semi-direct trains (non-shinkansen) leaving from Kyoto as well, so it may be better to take one of those to get closer to our final destination. Another option is to take the Special Rapid service on the JR Kyoto Line, which gets us to Osaka Station (note: not Shin-Osaka) - this station may have more direct connections to the AirBnB, but again, will require lugging around our belongings during transfers. To make things easier, we could also just take a few taxis from the Shin-Osaka station to the AirBnB, which I think might be our best option.
