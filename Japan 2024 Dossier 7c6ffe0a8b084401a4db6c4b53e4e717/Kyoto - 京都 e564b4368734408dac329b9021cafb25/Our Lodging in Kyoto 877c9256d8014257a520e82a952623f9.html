Our Lodging in Kyoto

Our AirBnB in Kyoto is a Kyomachiya, which a traditional Machiya-style townhouse found in originally in Kyoto (machiya are the traditional wooden row houses now found throughout Japan). These types of residences defined the architectural atmosphere of downtown Kyoto for centuries and, to this day, represent the standard defining form of the machiya throughout the country.
